📚 BETTING TIPS
📊
BANKROLL MANAGEMENT
Never stake more than 1–5% of your total bankroll on a single bet. This protects you from losing streaks and allows long-term sustainability.
🔍
RESEARCH BEFORE YOU BET
Study team form, head-to-head records, injuries, and conditions. Informed bettors consistently outperform impulsive ones.
🧮
VALUE BETTING
A bet has value when the real probability is higher than what the odds imply. Always compare odds across bookmakers.
📉
TRACK EVERY BET
Keep records of every wager. Over time, patterns show which markets are genuinely profitable.
🎯
SPECIALIZE
Focus on 1–2 leagues you truly know. A specialist consistently beats a casual bettor across many sports.
⚖️
AVOID ACCA TRAPS
House edge compounds with each selection. Singles and doubles offer far better long-term ROI than accumulators.
🛡️ ADDICTION PREVENTION
🚨
WARNING SIGNS
Betting more than you can afford, chasing losses, lying about gambling, neglecting responsibilities — these are serious warning signs. Act now.
📞 Gambling Helpline: 0800 000 000
🔒
SET HARD LIMITS
Decide a max loss before each session. Never borrow money to gamble. Never chase losses. Stop when you hit your limit.
💆
GAMBLING IS ENTERTAINMENT
Treat it as a hobby with a cost, not income. Protect your mental health and finances by maintaining this perspective.
